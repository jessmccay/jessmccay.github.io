About Me

Living with intention

For the past few years I've been doing one of the most important jobs there is — showing up for my family. I'm my dad's primary caregiver, which means I manage his meals, appointments, shopping, and finances with the same care and organization I once put into code.

I'm a San Diego girl at heart who loves nature, style, and the feeling of a space that's truly taken care of. These days I'm channeling that into a new direction: becoming a licensed home inspector. Because great homes, like great lives, are built on a solid foundation.

I'm also a mom, a fashion lover, a builder, and someone who believes you can reinvent yourself as many times as life calls for it.
